Here are some ways to stay safe online:

  • Always stop and think when you receive suspicious emails. Check for poor spelling and grammar, unusual email addresses, and vague or general subject headings.
  • Follow up with the sender over a different medium if you think the email is suspicious.
  • Avoid clicking links or opening attachments unless you are certain the sender is legitimate.
Remember: Always report suspicious emails to supervisors or the IT department.
